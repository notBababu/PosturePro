This Privacy Policy explains how The Desk Worker's Health Journal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit blog.fitnesscareshop.com.

We do not collect personal information such as your name or email address directly. However, the following data may be collected automatically when you visit this site:

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This site uses the following tracking technologies:

Meta Pixel (Facebook Pixel)
We use the Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of our advertising and to understand how visitors interact with this site. The Meta Pixel may set cookies and collect data about your visit, including pages viewed and actions taken. This data is shared with Meta Platforms, Inc. To learn more or opt out, visit Facebook's Privacy Policy or use their Ad Preferences tool.

Microsoft Clarity
We use Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use this site through session recordings and heatmaps. Clarity may set cookies and collect data about mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling behavior. This data is processed by Microsoft Corporation. To learn more, visit Microsoft's Privacy Statement.
